About

Adrian S. Haynes

/

While working in the music business, I became interested in web development after spending hours browsing venue, promoter and artist websites. What started as a passion project helping develop and design websites for up-and-coming artists, became a profession. Now I build websites for a range of clients including small businesses, non-profit organizations, and artists.

I am also a working musician and play in several groups in Austin and NYC. I love Buffy The Vampire Slayer, and have seen Lord Of The Rings: The Two Towers fourteen times in theaters.
