Hi! I am a front-end web developer and web designer. My goal is making clients' visions come to life by building eye-catching websites that are both engaging and user-friendly.

While working in the music business, I became interested in web development after spending hours browsing venue, promoter and artist websites. What started as a passion project helping develop and design websites for up-and-coming artists, became a profession. Now I build websites for a range of industries including tech, real estate, music, education, and non-profits.

I am also a working musician and play in several groups in Austin and NYC. I love Buffy The Vampire Slayer, and have seen Lord Of The Rings: The Two Towers fourteen times in theaters.

Skills & Expertise:

  • HTML/CSS/SASS
  • Bootstrap, Foundation
  • Wordpress
  • JavaScript/jQuery
  • Angular, React, Vue
  • PHP
